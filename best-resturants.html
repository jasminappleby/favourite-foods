Dominos

Slightly overpriced and probably one of the least Italian places ever. However, they do make some amazing pizza which is always spot on. Great customisation as well!

Location: UK

Dominos Website

Pizza Pilgrims

Pizza Pilgrims has bottomless pizza options (What is not to love!), but you can also buy pizza creation kits from them online! Gives you something to enjoy whilst self isolating.

Location: London

Pizza Pilgrims Website

Scream for Pizza

A very quirky pizza place with some authenic Italian classics. They also sell really cool merch on their website and have a very creative menu when ordering.

Location: Newcastle-Upon-Tyne

Scream for Pizzas Website

Icon Pizzeria

Some of the best pizza I have tried, giant portions and cheap prices. Straight off the pizza oven! If you are a Luton FC Season Ticket holder, you also get half price at the bar!

Location: Luton
